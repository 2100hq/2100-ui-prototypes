2100

In 2100, participants use virtual mining (proof of stake) to mint tokens that are linked to Twitter usernames. This process creates a unique commodity per Twitter account.

Each account is represented as an ERC-20 token and has a fixed supply of 2,100. Collectively, the top 10,000 Twitter accounts create an eventual supply of 21 million tokens.

The owner of an account on Twitter is entitled to 10% of their own token supply, and a market of virtual miners compete for the remaining 90%.

To mint 2100 tokens, participants stake DAI on the Twitter username of their choice. They forgo DAI denominated interest on platforms like Compound and earn shares of 2100 tokens instead.

2100 tokens have multiple kinds of utility. One kind of 2100 token utility is sybil-resistant Twitter subscriptions.

When your followers own your token, you can measure the strength of their interest and reward them in all kinds of interesting ways.

This creates value for your followers, value for your token, and value for you as a 10% shareholder in your own token, and 100% shareholder in your personal brand.
